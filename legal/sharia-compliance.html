Sharia Compliance Statement

Our exchange model is designed to follow Sharia principles. Key points:

  • No riba: No interest is charged or paid.
  • Agency (Wakīl): Customers may appoint us as agent at trade start; exchange finalises after funds clear and code delivery.
  • Transparency: Key rules appear before you confirm a trade.
  • Refund Right: Cancel before code delivery for a full refund in the same currency.

For certification, we engage external Sharia advisors and review our flows periodically.
