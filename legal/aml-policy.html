AML & CTF Policy

We are committed to detecting and preventing money laundering and terrorist financing.

Risk Assessment

We evaluate risks by customer type (retail or business), jurisdiction, payment method, and product category.

Customer Due Diligence

KYC checks may include ID verification, business registration, and source‑of‑funds where appropriate.

Monitoring & Reporting

We monitor for unusual activity and report suspicious matters to the relevant Maldives authorities in accordance with law.

Record‑Keeping

We retain records for at least the minimum period required by Maldives regulations.

Training

Staff receive periodic AML/CTF training and policy refreshers.
