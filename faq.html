Frequently Asked Questions

Are transactions Sharia‑compliant?

Yes. Our model avoids interest and hidden debt. You appoint us as your agent at trade start; the exchange finalises only when funds reach our bank and we deliver your code.

Which currencies do you accept?

We currently support MVR and USD. Details appear before checkout.

How fast is delivery?

Most orders complete within minutes during business hours, after payment clears.

Do you offer refunds?

You may cancel before code delivery for a full refund in the same currency. Once a code is delivered, refunds are generally not possible except where required by law.

Do you support corporate/reseller accounts?

Yes. See the B2B page for onboarding.

Any region restrictions?

Some gift cards are region‑locked by the issuer. We'll flag any restrictions before you complete an order.
